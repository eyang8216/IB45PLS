## Notes

- **Days 38–42 are buffer:** If you fell behind, use Days 38–40 to catch up on Acids/Bases or Redox. Organic Lessons 37–41 spill into the start of the course itself — that's fine; you'll have momentum.
- **Lessons 37–41 (Organic):** If you run out of days, organic is the most natural topic to carry into the school term — teachers typically start with it, so arriving with Lesson 36 done and 37 starting is perfect.
- **Re-take the diagnostic on Day 42.** Your first score was ~25%. Target for Day 42: 75%+. The jump will be dramatic because most of it was "never learned" rather than "can't learn."
- **Weekend flexibility:** If you miss a weekday, don't compress — just shift. Consistency matters more than speed.

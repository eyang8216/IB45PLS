Doppler sign convention for sound:

  • Observer moving toward source: $+v_o$ (numerator, top sign)
  • Observer moving away: $-v_o$ (numerator, bottom sign)
  • Source moving toward observer: $-v_s$ (denominator, bottom sign)
  • Source moving away: $+v_s$ (denominator, top sign)

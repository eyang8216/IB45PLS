## 1b. Uncertainty Propagation

These three rules are in the official data booklet:

| If... | Then... |
|-------|---------|
| $y = a \pm b$ | $\Delta y = \Delta a + \Delta b$ |
| $y = \frac{ab}{c}$ | $\frac{\Delta y}{y} = \frac{\Delta a}{a} + \frac{\Delta b}{b} + \frac{\Delta c}{c}$ |
| $y = a^n$ | $\frac{\Delta y}{y} = |n|\frac{\Delta a}{a}$ |

> **Also in booklet:** Vector components $A_H = A\cos\theta$, $A_V = A\sin\theta$

### C.5 Doppler Effect
| Description | Equation | Notes |
|-------------|----------|-------|
| Doppler (sound) | $f' = f\left(\frac{v \pm v_o}{v \mp v_s}\right)$ | See sign convention below |
| Doppler (light, $v \ll c$) | $\frac{\Delta\lambda}{\lambda} \approx \frac{v}{c}$ | HL |
| Hubble's Law | $v = H_0 d$ | HL |

**Doppler sign convention for sound:**
- Observer moving toward source: $+v_o$ (numerator, top sign)
- Observer moving away: $-v_o$ (numerator, bottom sign)
- Source moving toward observer: $-v_s$ (denominator, bottom sign)
- Source moving away: $+v_s$ (denominator, top sign)

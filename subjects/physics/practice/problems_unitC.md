## Section 1: Simple Harmonic Motion

### Question 1 [Paper 2 — 3 marks]

A block of mass $0.50\ \text{kg}$ is attached to a horizontal spring of spring constant $k = 200\ \text{N m}^{-1}$ on a frictionless surface. The block is displaced $4.0\ \text{cm}$ from equilibrium and released from rest at $t = 0$.

(a) Calculate the angular frequency $\omega$ of the oscillation. **[1 mark]**

(b) Calculate the maximum speed of the block. **[1 mark]**

(c) Calculate the total mechanical energy of the system. **[1 mark]**

---

### Question 2 [Paper 1 — 1 mark]

A. $\dfrac{T}{4}$

B. $\dfrac{T}{2}$

C. $T$

D. $2T$

**1.** If $(x - 2)^2 = 49$, what are all possible values of $x$?

A) $x = 7$ only
B) $x = -7$ only
C) $x = 9$ and $x = -5$
D) $x = 5$ and $x = -9$

**2.** The equation $f(x) = ax^2 + bx + c$ has vertex at $(3, -4)$. If $f(0) = 14$, what is the value of $a$?

A) $-2$
B) $2$
C) $\frac{1}{2}$
D) $-\frac{1}{2}$

---

**3.** A population of bacteria triples every 4 hours. If the initial population is 500, which expression gives the population after $t$ hours?

A) $500(3)^{t}$
B) $500(3)^{t/4}$
C) $500(3)^{4t}$
D) $500(4)^{t/3}$

---

**4.** The system of equations below has no solution. What is the value of $k$?
$$2x + 3y = 7$$
$$4x + ky = 10$$

A) 3
B) 6
C) $-3$
D) $-6$

**5.** If $\frac{x+1}{x-2} = \frac{4}{3}$, what is the value of $x$?

A) 5

C) 11
D) $-5$

**6.** A circle in the $xy$-plane has center $(h, k)$ and passes through the point $(0, 0)$. If the radius is $\sqrt{13}$, and $h = 2$, what is the value of $k$? (Assume $k > 0$.)

A) 1
B) 2
C) 3
D) 4

**7.** The function $g$ is defined by $g(x) = 2x^2 - 8x + 11$. What is the minimum value of $g(x)$?

**8.** In the $xy$-plane, line $\ell$ passes through $(1, 4)$ and is perpendicular to the line $y = \frac{1}{2}x + 3$. Which of the following is an equation of line $\ell$?

B) $y = -\frac{1}{2}x + \frac{9}{2}$
C) $y = 2x + 2$
D) $y = -2x + 2$

**9.** Let $f(x) = \sqrt{x + 5}$ and $g(x) = x^2 - 1$. What is $g(f(4))$?

**10.** The expression $\frac{x^2 - 5x + 6}{x^2 - 9}$ is equivalent to which of the following? (Assume $x \neq \pm 3$.)

A) $\frac{x - 2}{x + 3}$
B) $\frac{x - 2}{x - 3}$
C) $\frac{x + 2}{x - 3}$
D) $\frac{x - 3}{x + 3}$

**11.** A study surveyed 600 voters and found that 318 support Candidate A. The margin of error is $\pm 4\%$. Based on this, which is the best interpretation?

A) Candidate A will definitely win because 53% is a majority
B) Candidate A's support is between 49% and 57%, so a win is plausible but not certain
C) Exactly 53% of all voters support Candidate A
D) The survey is too close to call and should be discarded

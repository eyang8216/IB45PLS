**1.** If $3x + 7 = 4x - 5$, what is the value of $x$?

**2.** A car travels at a constant speed of 60 miles per hour. At this rate, how many miles will it travel in 2 hours and 15 minutes?

A) 120
B) 130
C) 135
D) 150

**3.** The equation $y = 2.5x + 30$ gives the total cost $y$, in dollars, of renting a kayak for $x$ hours. What is the meaning of the number 30 in this equation?

A) The cost per hour of renting the kayak
B) The total cost of renting the kayak
C) The fixed fee for renting the kayak, regardless of hours
D) The number of hours the kayak can be rented

B) 3
C) 4
D) 5

B) 8
C) 9
D) 18

**6.** The scatterplot below shows the relationship between hours of sleep and test performance for 20 students. The line of best fit has equation $y = 5.2x + 48$. According to this model, what is the predicted test score for a student who slept 8 hours?

A) 48.0
B) 89.6
C) 84.0
D) 41.6

**7.** A store reduces the price of a jacket by 30% for a sale. The sale price is $56. What was the original price?

D) $186.67

**8.** What are the solutions to $x^2 - 7x + 12 = 0$?

A) $x = -3$ and $x = -4$

C) $x = 3$ and $x = 4$
D) $x = -3$ and $x = 4$

**9.** The function $f(x) = 500(0.85)^x$ models the value of a computer $x$ years after purchase. What does 0.85 represent?

A) The computer loses 85% of its value each year
B) The computer retains 85% of its value each year
C) The computer's value is $85 after 1 year
D) The computer loses 15% of its value after $x$ years

**12.** In a survey of 400 randomly selected adults, 64% said they exercise at least twice per week. The margin of error is $\pm 5\%$. Which of the following is a valid conclusion?

A) Exactly 64% of all adults exercise at least twice per week
B) More than 70% of all adults exercise at least twice per week
C) It is plausible that between 59% and 69% of all adults exercise at least twice per week

### Question 1

In the equation below, $k$ is a constant:

$$\frac{2x + k}{x - 3} = \frac{5x + 1}{x - 3} + 4$$

For what value of $k$ does the equation have no solution?

A) $-10$
B) $13$
C) $-11$
D) $-1$

### Question 2

The table below shows the distribution of students at a high school by grade and whether they participate in at least one sport.

| Grade | Play Sport | Do Not Play Sport | Total |
|-------|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| 9th | 84 | 56 | 140 |
| 10th | 96 | 44 | 140 |
| 11th | 72 | 68 | 140 |
| 12th | 60 | 80 | 140 |
| **Total** | 312 | 248 | 560 |

If a student who plays a sport is selected at random, what is the probability that the student is in 11th or 12th grade?

A) $\frac{132}{560}$
B) $\frac{132}{312}$
C) $\frac{72}{312}$
D) $\frac{140}{312}$

### Question 3

B) $2a^2 - 4a - 2$
C) $-2a^2 + 4a - 2$
D) $-2a^2 + 4a + 2$

### Question 6

### Question 8

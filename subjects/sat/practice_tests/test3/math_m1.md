### Question 3

The table below shows the number of hours four students spent studying for a test and their scores.

| Student | Hours Studied | Test Score |
|---------|:---:|:---:|
| A | 2 | 68 |
| B | 4 | 76 |
| C | 5 | 82 |
| D | 7 | 94 |

Based on the data, which statement is best supported?

A) Each additional hour of study guarantees exactly 4 points higher on the test.
B) There is a positive association between hours studied and test score.
C) Student C studied exactly twice as long as Student A and scored exactly twice as high.
D) There is no relationship between hours studied and test score.
